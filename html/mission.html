Our Mission

Empowering Individuals

Iron Fox Forensics is dedicated to supporting anyone who has had their cryptocurrency stolen, whether through malware, phishing, or other means. We ensure that justice and blockchain security are within reach for everyone, regardless of their circumstances.

Comprehensive Reporting

Our app provides detailed reports that track where stolen funds have gone, identify potential cash-out locations, and uncover other critical insights. These reports empower users with the information they need to take action.

Setting a New Standard for Blockchain Security

We are committed to redefining blockchain security as transparent, inclusive, and open to all. At Iron Fox Forensics, we believe that the promise of blockchain’s openness should extend to its security, ensuring that no one is left behind.
